About

What is GojiraTuners?

GojiraTuners is an online tuning shop that focuses on selling things to make your GTR aka "Gojira" stronger to defeat other monster cars!
Nissan GTR is widely known as Godzilla or Gojira, originating in the Australian media as a nod to the fictional Japanese movie monster that destroyed everything in its path, but also forced the Australian Touring Car Championship to ditch the global Group A regulations.
